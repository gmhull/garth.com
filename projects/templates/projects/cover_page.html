ABOUT ME

G101: Intro to Garth

In 2016 I started studying Mechanical Engineering at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. Four years later I left with my BS and MS degrees, and a desire to keep learning. Now I am a full time engineer with a knack for problem solving and mechanical design.

WPI offered some amazing experiences. I patented a mobility walker, jumped pretty high (high jump), and met a bunch of fun nerds. One of the coolest was traveling accross the world to Albania to work with a non-govermental organization for 3 months. We were able to tour the country and experience the culture, history, and (most importantly) the food.

When I'm not outside, I spend my time on a variety of creative outlets. This website is one of them and holds some others. Explore some of the different robotics, programming, and engineering design projects I have worked on.
